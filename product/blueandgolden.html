Blue And Golden Lithe Dancer

Details (SB05Z700)

Description: This oversize watch is dedicated to the Year of the Snake. It features a dark blue dial with multi-colored print, gold-colored hour and minute hands that glow in the dark, an orange seconds hand and a dark blue day-date window with white print at 3 o'clock. The matte dark blue biosourced case features biosourced glass and a matte dark blue biosourced bezel with gold-colored print and a matte orange pusher. The matte dark blue strap with multi-colored print is paired with gold-colored loops with silver-colored print and a gold-colored half-buckle.
